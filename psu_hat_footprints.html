R1, R2 DIN0207 body is 6.3 × 2.5mm. Laid flat at 7.62mm pitch leaves ~0.65mm clearance each side. Standard ¼W metal film resistor fits this footprint from any supplier.
C1, C4 5mm disc ceramic. D5.0mm = disc diameter, W2.5mm = body width, P2.50mm = lead pitch. Covers 100nF in any voltage rating (50V, 100V) — all have the same package.
C3, C5 Radial electrolytic, 5mm diameter body. Lead pitch of 2.0mm is typical for 10µF 25V caps — verify against your specific DigiKey part datasheet as some use 1.5mm pitch.
C2 8mm diameter body, 3.5mm lead pitch. Standard for 220µF 25V radial electrolytics from most manufacturers (Panasonic EEU-FR1E221, Nichicon UPW series, etc.).
C6 8mm diameter body, 5.0mm lead pitch. Low-ESR caps (EEU-FR1C471B) use wider pitch than standard electrolytics of the same body size — this is why C6 has a different footprint to C2 despite both being 8mm caps.
Always verify lead pitch against your actual component datasheet before ordering PCBs.
Electrolytic capacitors in particular vary by manufacturer and series. A 0.5mm pitch error means the cap physically won't fit the footprint. Check the "Lead Spacing" or "Lead Pitch" field on the DigiKey part page.
